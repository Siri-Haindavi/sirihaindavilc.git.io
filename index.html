Behind the Scenes at Les Crêpes

Welcome to Les Crêpes, where tradition meets innovation in a cozy corner of Kitchener. Our story began with a simple yet ambitious dream: to bring a slice of the French countryside to the heart of the city, one crepe at a time. In our kitchen, the aroma of butter sizzles on the griddle, and the air carries the scent of sweet and savory delights, weaving the very essence of France into our local fabric. Our crepes are more than just food; they are folded with passion, filled with quality local and ingredients, and served with a side of conviviality. At Les Crêpes, we cherish the art of fine dining as much as the joy of a casual bite. Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a lingering brunch, we invite you to find a spot, take a moment, and savor the flavors carefully crafted by our skilled chefs. From the classic 'Nutella Crepe' to our innovative 'Cheese & Ham Crepe', there's a story in every bite.


Our doors opened with the vision of creating a space where people could gather, pause from the hustle of daily life, and enjoy the simple pleasures. We are more than a crêperie; we are a retreat, a place where every customer is treated like a guest in our home. So come in, pull up a chair, and let us transport you to the streets of Paris with the perfect crepe, paired with a cup of our finest brew. Because here at Les Crêpes, we believe that every meal should be an experience, a memory to cherish, and a taste of joy to carry with you.
